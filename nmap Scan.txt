sudo Syn Scans require root permissions on Linux
-sS Syn Scan - Faster than a regular ping scan
-p- All 65535 TCP Ports
-n Skips the DNS resolution
-Pn Assume the host is online (Skips initial online check)
--min-rate=9362 Scan a minimum of 9362 ports at once (Chosen since it would be around 1 second faster)
