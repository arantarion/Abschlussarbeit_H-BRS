sudo nmap -sS -p- -n -Pn --min-rate=9362 ipHere


sudo Syn Scans require root permissions on Linux
-sS Syn Scan - Faster than a regular ping scan
-p- All 65535 TCP Ports
-n Skips the DNS resolution
-Pn Assume the host is online (Skips initial online check)
--min-rate=9362 Scan a minimum of 9362 ports at once (Chosen since it would be around 1 second faster)

docker run --rm -it instrumentisto/nmap -sS -p- -n -Pn --min-rate=9362 192.168.1.1

nmap -sSCV -p- -oN <dateiname>.txt 192.168.1.1

nmap -sC -sS -sV -p- 192.168.1.1

-sC: equivalent to --script=default
-sS/sT/sA/sW/sM: TCP SYN/Connect()/ACK/Window/Maimon scans
sV: Probe open ports to determine service/version info

-T Timing template (higher is faster)
